Midsummer

After I had visited Stockholm, I went out to the countryside to learn about the event Midsummer. Midsummer is a feast celebrated between the 19 and 25 of June. People pick flowers and make wreaths on a may pole to celebrate the feast. Some food eaten includes pickled herring, boiled new potatoes with dill, soured cream and chives, and a grilled dish.
