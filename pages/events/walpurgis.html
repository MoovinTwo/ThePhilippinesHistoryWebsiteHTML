Walpurgis Night

After I had learned about Midsummer, I learned about Walpurgis Night. Walpurgis Night is a celebration that takes place on April 30th and is used as a kickoff for the spring. People gather around a bonfire lit around dusk, and after the fire dies, people move to restaurants and pubs. People often sing for this event as a way to welcome spring into the scenery.
