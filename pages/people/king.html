Gustavus Adolphus

After I had visited IKEA, I had gone to Stockholm to learn about Gustavus Adolphus. Gustavus was born on December 9, 1594, and died on November 6, 1632. He reigned from 1611 to 1631. When he had entered the throne, he had three wars and a constitutional crisis that he had to deal with. To end the war with Denmark, he had given their North Seaport to Denmark to be peaceful terms
