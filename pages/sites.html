The Kebnekaise is a mountain range in norther Sweden that is located 103 miles from the Arctic Circle.

The Kebnekaise Mountain Range

After my visit to Kalmar, I went to the Kebnekaise Mountain Range to see the beautiful mountains. Kebnekaise is located in the county of Norrbotten, northern Sweden, roughly 25 miles from the Norwegian border. I had learned from a local hiker that the mountain range covers about 200 square miles. The range is mostly desolate with remains of birch forests.

  1. Kebnekaise is located in Norrbotten, norther Sweden
  2. Kebnekaise is 25 miles from the Norwegian Border and 103 miles from the arctic circle
  3. The range covers about 200 square miles
  4. The range is mostly desolate with some stubs of a birch forest
